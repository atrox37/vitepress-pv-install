### Step Details

#### 1. Combined Frame and Short Column Combination

1. First align and lay flat the two short column weldments, then place the combined frame on top of the short column weldments and adjust until the installation holes are aligned

Note: The rail installation plane faces down and aligns with the holes on the short column installation plane.
